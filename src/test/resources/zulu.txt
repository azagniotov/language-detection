USuku luka Khisimusi isikhathi esiletha injabulo kubantu abaningi. Lesi sikhathi senza ukuba kube khona ukuhlangana nemindeni okudala yagcinana, kuphunyulwe, futhi kuncokolwe. Kubazalwane ukhisimuzi usho usuku lapho uJesu Khrestu azalwa ngalo.Uma sokusondele lesi sikhathi sibona ngemihlobiso emihle futhi ehlukahlukene emigwaqeni, ezitolo, nasemakhaya.
