Nhoroondo kunyorwa pasi nekutaurwa kwezviitiko zvakakosha zvakaitika mudunhu, nyika kana Pasirose. Nhoroondo yakakosha kurangarira kuti vanhu vazive. Kunyorwa kwenhoroondo enyika chinhu chakakosha asiwo chinogona kuva nemakaktanwa nokuti avo vanonyora vanokwanisa kuva nedivi ravanorerekera. Kazhinji pane pane mapoka evanhu vanoita rondedzero yenhoroondo inoteererwa yogonyorwa pasi, zvinotaurwa nevamwe zvichisiiwa. Izvi zvisingarevi kuti zvasiyiwa hazvina kukosha kana kuti hazvina basa. Mukunyorwa kwenhoroondo zvinodaro kuti vanhu vanotevera ndivo vanonzwikwa kunyanya
